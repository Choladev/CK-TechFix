About CK TechFix

Who We Are

CK TechFix is a community-based repair service founded by a young Zambian tech entrepreneur with a passion for solving technology problems. We started with a small workshop in Ngombe and have grown to serve all of Lusaka with honest, skilled, and affordable tech repair services.

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and affordable tech repair services while building lasting relationships with our customers through transparency and exceptional service.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted tech repair service in Zambia, known for quality workmanship, customer education, and community involvement.
